Who are we?

The Maxwell society is the Physics department's student society. Discover the current committee.

What do we do?

We hold a weekly lecture series with physicists from around the country and host a wide variety of social events. We also publish a monthly newsletter to keep you informed of what is going on.

How to join?

All King's students are entitled to be members of the Society by paying an annual membership fee of £5. Join now!

Join us

By giving us your email we can send you our newsletters and a link to pay through KCLSU. Please also follow us on Twitter and Facebook.

Regular Maxwell Society events are:

  • The Monday Seminar Series: A lecture series on a range of science topics are given by speakers invited from around the country as well as the King's Physics Department. A list of upcoming events can be found here.
  • The Cumberland Lodge Weekend: This residential weekend takes place near the end of February and is organised around a theme. This is a great opportunity to relax with friends and get to know the faculty better.
